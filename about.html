How it works

Our programme offers free services to any person that desires to establish their own business.This programme has two categories of applicants. applicants can either login or sign up as a mentor or a mentee.A mentor provides clear guidance to mentees by providing reqular sessions of business mentorship.The mentor partners with the mentee one to one ensuring that they attain the best results.The mentee follows guidance given by the mentor.The mentee is allowed to enquire on anything that they haven't understood.Furthrmore the mentee should avail time in order to fulfill reqular checkups.Once you have submitted your mentoring request your mentor will reach out to you via email to schedule appointment sessions.Sessions include different communication methods incluing phone call,video call,email and even one on one meetings.During sessions the mentor will ask various questions to provide an overview of current situation.To make the most out of your mentoring session actively participate in the session.
